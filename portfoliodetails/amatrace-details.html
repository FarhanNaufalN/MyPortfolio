AMATRACE APP

Project information

  • Category: CRUD Management Traceability System
  • Client: AMATI Indonesia
  • Project Year: 2024
  • Project URL: https://github.com/FarhanNaufalN/AMATrace

Traceability Platform is a technology designed to meet the need for product transparency by tracking and verifying the journey of products through each stage of the supply chain using technologies such as QR Code and Machine Learning to collect and manage data on the movement of goods. QR Code collects real-time data, while Machine Learning analyzes the data to increase efficiency for MSME stock needs. The benefits of Traceability Platform include increased transparency, which increases consumer trust, the ability to detect stock needs efficiently, and helps companies comply with international regulations and standards.

  • Supply Chain
  • Inventory Management
  • User Administration
  • CRUD Management
  • RESTful API
