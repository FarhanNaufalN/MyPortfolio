Moody App

Project information

  • Category: Entertainment App
  • Client: Dicoding
  • Project Year: 2024

Moody App is an entertainment application that provides a wide variety of the latest movies and TV series from around the world. By utilizing RESTful API technology, Moody App allows users to enjoy a catalog of content that is continuously updated in real-time. Users can easily discover their favorite movies and series from various genres, such as action, drama, comedy, animation, and documentaries. Additionally, the app offers personalized content recommendations based on users' preferences, making every viewing experience more enjoyable. Moody App provides complete information for each movie and series, including synopses, cast lists, user reviews, and official trailers. With an intuitive interface, users can quickly and efficiently search for content through an advanced search feature. The app also includes bookmark and watchlist features, allowing users to save movies or series they want to watch later. This feature is particularly useful for those who have a long list of favorite shows to watch.
